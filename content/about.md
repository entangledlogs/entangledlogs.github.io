## Why this blog exists?

The idea of the blog is to feature some random collection of notes, thoughts, and logs as I get to explore random things in a wide variety of subjects. I want these notes to link me somewhere.

## Why is it named "Entangled Logs"?

I want this blog to be like a collection of notes, logs, and journals. As I try random things that span multiple domains, the name expects to capture these ideas shared between them.
